Accepted Items for Recycling

We accept the following types of electronics for safe and eco-friendly recycling:

  • Computers & Laptops
  • Servers & Networking Equipment
  • Cell Phones & Tablets
  • Printers, Copiers & Scanners
  • Monitors & Displays
  • Telecommunication Equipment
  • Computer Components (Motherboards, Hard Drives, etc.)
  • TVs (Flat Screen Only)
  • Batteries (Lithium-ion, Lead-acid)
  • Miscellaneous Electronics (Cables, Keyboards, Mice, etc.)

Note: We do not accept items such as appliances, light bulbs, or hazardous materials.
